Our Story

Import AutoCare was founded in 1996 with a dual purpose: to provide fulfilling, rewarding careers in the automotive industry and to deliver exceptional car care to the Cuyahoga Falls community. We believe that when we invest in our team, our team invests in your vehicle. It's a people-first philosophy that drives everything we do.

Over the decades, we've grown into the most trusted Asian import repair shop in Summit County. Our technicians are trained specifically on Japanese, Korean, and other Asian-engineered vehicles — understanding the unique specifications, common issues, and best practices for every make and model we service.

Our Mission

To redefine the auto repair experience through transparency, convenience, quality, and integrity. We call it the people-first approach — because your trust matters more to us than any single transaction.

Why Asian Import Specialists?

Asian import vehicles — Toyota, Honda, Nissan, Subaru, Mazda, Hyundai, Kia, Lexus, Acura, Infiniti, and more — are engineered with specific tolerances, fluids, and procedures that differ from domestic vehicles. A generalist shop may not have the training or equipment to service these vehicles correctly. Our team does.

By specializing in Asian imports, we've developed a depth of knowledge and a suite of diagnostic tools that allow us to solve problems faster and more accurately than a general repair shop — saving you time and money.

We're proud to be part of the Cuyahoga Falls and greater Summit County community. We sponsor local events, support charities, and are deeply committed to the neighborhoods we serve. When you choose Import AutoCare, you're supporting a local, family-oriented business that cares about its neighbors.
