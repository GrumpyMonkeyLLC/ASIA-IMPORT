FAQ Library

Answers to the most common questions from our customers.

Home › FAQ

Have a question about your vehicle or our services? Browse our FAQ below. Can't find your answer? Give us a call — we're always happy to help.

Do you only work on Asian import vehicles?

We specialize in Asian import vehicles including Toyota, Honda, Nissan, Subaru, Mazda, Hyundai, Kia, Lexus, Acura, Infiniti, Mitsubishi, and Suzuki. By focusing on these makes, our technicians develop a deep expertise that results in faster, more accurate diagnoses and repairs.

How long will my repair take?

Repair times vary greatly depending on the type of service. Many routine maintenance services (oil changes, brake pads) can be completed the same day, often within a couple of hours. More complex repairs may require additional time. We'll always give you an estimated completion time when you drop off your vehicle, and we'll communicate promptly if anything changes.

Do you provide estimates before starting work?

Absolutely. We always provide a clear, written estimate before any work begins. We won't start a repair without your approval. If we discover additional issues during the repair, we'll contact you immediately with a revised estimate — no surprises.

What does your warranty cover?

Our warranty covers parts and labor for repairs performed at our shop for 24 months or 24,000 miles, whichever comes first. The warranty is honored nationwide at participating repair facilities. For full details, visit our Warranty page.

Why is my check engine light on?

A check engine light can indicate a wide range of issues — from something as minor as a loose gas cap to a more serious engine or emissions problem. Our technicians use advanced diagnostic equipment to read the fault codes from your vehicle's computer and identify the root cause quickly and accurately.

How often should I change my oil?

The old "every 3,000 miles" rule no longer applies to most modern vehicles. Many Asian imports running full-synthetic oil can go 5,000–10,000 miles between oil changes. We recommend following your vehicle manufacturer's recommended interval, which you'll find in your owner's manual — or just ask us and we'll advise based on your specific vehicle and driving habits.

Do you offer towing service?

Yes! If your vehicle can't make it to our shop under its own power, we can help arrange towing. Visit our Towing Service page or call us at 330 929 6360 for assistance.

Do you offer financing for repairs?

Yes. We offer financing options to help spread the cost of unexpected repairs into manageable monthly payments. Ask our service advisor for details, or visit our Financing page.

What are your hours of operation?

We're open Monday through Thursday from 8:00 AM to 5:30 PM, and Friday from 8:00 AM to 2:00 PM. We are closed on weekends.

Where are you located?

We're located at 2121 4th Street, Cuyahoga Falls, OH 44221 — conveniently serving Cuyahoga Falls, Akron, and the greater Summit County area. Get directions on Google Maps.

Can I wait at the shop while my car is being serviced?

Yes! We have a comfortable waiting area for customers. For longer repairs, we're happy to discuss other transportation arrangements as needed.

Are you hiring?

We're often looking for qualified automotive professionals who share our people-first values. Visit our Team Application page to learn more and apply.

Still have a question?

Our team is just a phone call away. We're happy to answer any question about your vehicle, our services, or your upcoming visit.
