Import AutoCare prioritizes quality and assurance in every repair. We stand behind our work with a comprehensive warranty on both parts and labor — so your peace of mind extends far beyond our shop in Cuyahoga Falls.

What's Covered

Our warranty covers the parts and labor for the specific repairs performed at our shop. If a covered repair fails within the warranty period, we will make it right — at no additional cost to you.

  • ✔ Parts supplied and installed by Import AutoCare
  • ✔ Labor charges for covered repairs
  • ✔ All major systems: engine, transmission, brakes, suspension, electrical, A/C, and more
  • ✔ Honored at any participating repair facility in the nationwide warranty network

Nationwide Coverage

One of the most valuable aspects of our warranty is that it travels with you. Whether you're driving across Ohio or across the country, our warranty is honored at thousands of participating repair shops in our nationwide network. You're never left stranded without support.

How to Use Your Warranty

  1. Keep your repair invoice. It serves as your warranty documentation.
  2. Contact us first. Call us at 330 929 6360 and we'll help coordinate any warranty claim.
  3. If you're out of town, locate a participating shop in the nationwide network and provide them with your warranty documentation.

Warranty Exclusions

Our warranty does not cover normal wear items (brake pads, wiper blades, filters used in the normal course of maintenance), damage caused by accidents, misuse, or neglect, or repairs performed by other shops after leaving our facility. Full terms are available upon request.

Questions about your warranty?

Call us and we'll walk you through exactly what's covered for your specific repair. We're here to help.
