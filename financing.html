Financing Plans

Don't let an unexpected repair cost keep you off the road.

Home › Financing

At Import AutoCare, we understand that car repairs can come at inconvenient times. Our financing options are here to make sure cost never stands between you and a safe, reliable vehicle.

Why Finance Your Repair?

Safety should never be delayed because of budget concerns. Whether it's a brake job, a major engine repair, or an unexpected transmission issue, our financing options allow you to get the work done now and pay over time with manageable monthly payments.

💳

Flexible Terms

Choose a payment plan that fits your budget. Multiple term lengths available to keep monthly payments affordable.

⚡

Fast Approval

Quick application process so you don't have to wait. In many cases, you'll have an answer within minutes.

🔒

Secure & Safe

Your personal and financial information is kept private and secure throughout the application process.

🚗

Get Back on the Road

Approved financing means we can begin your repair immediately. No waiting, no delays.

How It Works

  1. Get your estimate. Bring your vehicle in or call us. We'll provide a clear, up-front estimate of the repair costs.
  2. Apply for financing. Ask our service advisor about our financing options. The application is simple and takes just a few minutes.
  3. Get approved. Most customers receive a credit decision quickly. Once approved, we'll move forward with your repair.
  4. Drive away. Your vehicle is repaired and you pay over time in convenient monthly installments.

Ready to get started?

Call us or stop by the shop to ask about our current financing options. Our team is happy to walk you through the details and get you on the road.
