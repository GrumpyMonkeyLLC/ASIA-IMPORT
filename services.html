// What We Do //

Expert Auto Services

Complete Asian import repair by skilled technicians in Cuyahoga Falls, OH.

Home › Services

The team at Import AutoCare is committed to providing top-notch services to keep your vehicle running smoothly. From routine maintenance to complex engine diagnostics, we have the skills and experience to tackle any automotive challenge. We prioritize your safety and satisfaction, delivering reliable and affordable services you can count on.

🔧

Oil Changes & Maintenance

Regular oil changes and preventive maintenance to keep your engine running clean and efficient. We use quality oil and filters matched to your vehicle's specs.

🛑

Brake Service & Repair

From brake pad replacement to full rotor and caliper service, we ensure your vehicle stops safely every time. We inspect your entire brake system with every visit.

🔍

Engine Diagnostics

Advanced computer diagnostics to pinpoint the exact cause of check engine lights, performance issues, and warning codes on all Asian import vehicles.

⚙️

Transmission Service

Automatic and manual transmission fluid services, diagnostics, and repairs to keep your drivetrain shifting smoothly for miles to come.

❄️

A/C & Heating

We service, repair, and recharge air conditioning systems and inspect heating components so you stay comfortable in every season.

🔋

Battery & Electrical

Battery testing, replacement, and full electrical diagnostics. We trace shorts, fix charging issues, and keep your vehicle's electronics running right.

🛞

Tires & Alignment

Tire rotation, balancing, and wheel alignment services to maximize tire life, improve handling, and keep your vehicle tracking straight.

💧

Fluid Services

Coolant, power steering, brake fluid, and differential fluid services. We inspect and top off all vital fluids to keep your systems protected.

🔩

Suspension & Steering

Struts, shocks, tie rods, ball joints, and steering rack service. We diagnose handling issues and restore a smooth, controlled ride.

💨

Exhaust & Emissions

Muffler repair, catalytic converter service, and emissions testing support. We fix exhaust leaks and help your vehicle pass Ohio emissions requirements.

🔦

Timing Belt & Chain

Proper timing belt and chain replacement at the manufacturer-recommended intervals to prevent catastrophic engine failure on your import vehicle.

🚗

Pre-Purchase Inspections

Thinking of buying a used Asian import? Let us perform a thorough inspection before you commit, so you know exactly what you're getting.

Not sure what your car needs?

Give us a call and one of our expert technicians will help diagnose your concern and recommend the right service — no pressure, no guesswork.
